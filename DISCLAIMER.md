# Disclaimer

PulsePoint Alert Monitor is a supplemental awareness and backup alerting aid only.

It is **not**:

- an official dispatch system,
- a pager,
- a CAD terminal,
- an MDT,
- a radio,
- a station alerting system,
- a life-safety system,
- a guaranteed real-time alerting mechanism.

PulsePoint feeds may lag, omit incidents, omit units, change format, become unavailable, or fail. This tool may also fail due to operating system sleep, network connectivity, browser changes, API/provider failures, local configuration errors, or software defects.

Do not rely on this software as your sole or primary means of receiving emergency calls or dispatch information. Users are responsible for complying with agency policies, local law, operational requirements, and any applicable terms of service.
